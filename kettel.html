Electric Kettle - Realistic Illustration













DESIGN PROBLEM: To design ads for the target audience. To illustrate the company product qualities and features. To instill the idea that convenience does not equate to lack of elegance and luxury. To make the ads outstanding and creative. DESIGN PROCESS: I picked one of Pukomc most popular product which is their electric kettle. Using illustrator I created a realistic duplication of the kettle. I researched Pukomc products and overview it qualities, features and previous ads created.
